About Tentrite

Leading the digital marketplace revolution

Our Story

Founded in 2019, Tentrite emerged from a simple observation: buying and selling websites was unnecessarily complicated and expensive. Our founders, experienced entrepreneurs and web developers, saw an opportunity to disrupt the market by creating a platform that prioritized simplicity, affordability, and security. What started as a passion project has grown into the world's most trusted website marketplace, serving thousands of buyers and sellers across more than 150 countries.

Over the past five years, we've facilitated the sale of websites generating over $500 million in combined annual revenue. Our success is built on the foundation of trust, transparency, and an unwavering commitment to our users' success. Every feature we build, every decision we make, is guided by our mission to make digital asset trading accessible to everyone.

Our Mission

At Tentrite, our mission is to democratize digital asset trading by providing a secure, transparent, and user-friendly marketplace where anyone can buy or sell websites, apps, and online businesses. We believe that digital assets represent real value and opportunity, and that everyone deserves access to a fair, efficient marketplace to exchange them.

We're committed to innovation, security, and customer success. Our core values—integrity, transparency, and excellence—guide everything we do. We invest heavily in technology, security measures, and customer support to ensure that every transaction on our platform is smooth, secure, and rewarding for both buyers and sellers. Together, we're building the future of digital commerce.
